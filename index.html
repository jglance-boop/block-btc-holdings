Acquisition Timeline

Oct 8, 2020
+4,709 BTC Initial Purchase
$50M investment at ~$10,618 per BTC. Block (then Square) becomes one of the first public companies to hold Bitcoin on its balance sheet.
Feb 23, 2021
+3,318 BTC
$170M investment at ~$51,236 per BTC. Total holdings reach ~8,027 BTC.
2021
Lightning node launched Lightning
Block begins operating a Lightning Network node, routing both Cash App Lightning payments and external traffic. Node earns routing fees in sats, accumulating ~11 BTC over 2021–2023.
2021 – 2023
~8,027 BTC (held steady)
No major purchases during this period. Block holds through the bear market while Lightning node quietly accumulates routing yield.
Apr 2024
DCA program begins Strategy Shift
Block starts investing 10% of monthly gross profit from Bitcoin products into BTC purchases via dollar-cost averaging. Lightning node yield also begins being formally tracked in the MBR.
Q2 2024
DCA: +173 BTC | LN Yield: ~$15K
Holdings reach 8,211 BTC. Lightning node generates ~$15K in routing fees (Cash + External channels).
Q3 2024
DCA: +151 BTC | LN Yield: ~$70K (+1 BTC)
Holdings reach 8,363 BTC. Lightning yield accelerates as routing volume grows. Node processes both internal Cash App payments and external routing.
Q4 2024
DCA: +120 BTC | LN Yield: ~$131K (+2 BTC)
Holdings reach 8,485 BTC. Lightning yield hits ~$52K/month in December as BTC price and network activity surge.
